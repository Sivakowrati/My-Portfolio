About Urs Beauty Hub

Professional Beauty Services Come to Your Doorstep

Who We Are

Urs Beauty Hub is a premier professional beauty service provider, bringing the luxury of a salon directly to your home. Our mission is to make premium beauty treatments accessible, hygienic, and convenient. With a team of trained and experienced beauty experts, we ensure every service is delivered with care and precision.

Our At-Home Salon Services

We offer a wide range of professional beauty services in the comfort of your home:

  • Facials & Skincare treatments for glowing, healthy skin
  • Haircuts, styling, smoothening, and coloring services
  • Bridal and party makeup services
  • Manicure, pedicure, waxing, and spa services
  • Personalized grooming and wellness treatments
Each service is performed using top-quality, hygienic products to ensure a safe and relaxing experience.

Where We Serve

Urs Beauty Hub currently provides services in Mumbai and Thane districts, with focus on the following areas:

  • Kalyan: Kalyan East & West, including all local neighborhoods.
  • Navi Mumbai: Vashi, Nerul, Kharghar, Panvel, and surrounding locations.
